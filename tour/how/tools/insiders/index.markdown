<p>This page provides an overview of the report Insiders, complete with info summary cards for each of the Insiders. To access the summary view of any Insider, click on the Insider’s name. To close the summary view, click anywhere outside of it.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.15.33_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2013" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.15.33_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.15.33_AM.jpeg" width="690" height="514" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">Sorting by Type</h3>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Topic</span></h4>
<p>Sorts Insiders by the topic, from highest to lowest Insider score.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.16.11_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2014" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.16.11_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.16.11_AM.jpeg" width="690" height="253" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Emerging Insiders</span></h4>
<p>Emerging insiders are the people who have been on Twitter for the least amount of time but have already been followed by a substantial number of other insiders.  These are the community approved up-and-coming specialists.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.39.33_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2015" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.39.33_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.39.33_AM.jpeg" width="690" height="381" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Listeners</span></h4>
<p>Listeners are the insiders in a topic community who “listen to” (follow) the most other insiders.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.52.53_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2016" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.52.53_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_9.52.53_AM.jpeg" width="741" height="383" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Most Followed Insiders</span></h4>
<p>Most Followed are the Insiders with the greatest number of followers.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.16.31_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2017" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.16.31_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.16.31_AM.jpeg" width="697" height="255" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Oldest Accounts</span></h4>
<p>Oldest accounts are those Insiders who have been on Twitter the longest.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.22.10_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2018" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.22.10_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.22.10_AM.jpeg" width="692" height="246" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4 dir="ltr"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Newest Accounts</span></h4>
<p dir="ltr">Newest accounts are simply those which have most recently joined Twitter. They are part of the community and register as an Insider already, but this filter is not specific to their level of influence.</p>
<p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.35.44_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2019" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.35.44_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.35.44_AM.jpeg" width="693" height="247" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Sort by Most Active Insiders</span></h4>
<p>Most active are the Insiders who tweet at the highest rate (i.e. highest average number of tweets/day).</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.27.36_AM.jpeg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-2021" alt="Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.27.36_AM" src="http://getlittlebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen_Shot_2014-02-18_at_10.27.36_AM.jpeg" width="692" height="251" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Sorting by Network</h3>
<p>Use the 'Network' drop-down menu to sort Insiders according to which other social media networks they are on. We identify Insiders across  networks including Facebook, LinkedIn, Youtube, Google+ and Slideshare.  You can also click on any of the Insiders' names to view a pop-up card of additional information about that Insider, including icons that denote which other social networks we've discovered that Insider on.</p>
